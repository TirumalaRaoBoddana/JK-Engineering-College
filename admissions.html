Eligibility Criteria

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) expand_more
  • Completed high school with a minimum of 60% in science stream.
  • Must have studied Physics, Chemistry, and either Mathematics or Biology.
  • Minimum age of 17 years at the time of admission.
Bachelor of Arts (B.A.) expand_more
  • Completed high school from any recognized board.
  • Minimum aggregate of 50% in qualifying examinations.
  • No specific subject combination required.
Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) expand_more
  • Completed high school, preferably in the commerce stream.
  • Minimum aggregate of 55% with Mathematics/Economics as a subject.
  • Applicants from other streams may be subject to an aptitude test.
