About the Department

Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. Our mission is to advance the frontiers of computing and to educate the next generation of leaders in technology and research through a rigorous, hands-on curriculum.

Founded in 1985, our department has a rich history of innovation, consistently ranking among the top computer science programs in the nation. We are committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive environment where creativity and collaboration thrive.
